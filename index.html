Background

As I graduated from the Institute of foreign languages, I was actually immersed in the world of linguistics, making translations.

The biggest part of my life I was involved in activities quite different from those I am doing now.

Most of all, I liked dynamical part of my work which embraced such activities as visiting different NGOs and governmental institutions, meeting different people and learning something new every day.

Childhood dreams

As a child, I had been dreaming about becoming a doctor/physician, just like my grandfather.

As a teenager, I used to read many of his sophisticated books, finding pleasure in what other people would sometimes regard as disgusting and uninteresting and dreaming of how I was gonna help other people in the future.

Reality

The times have changed, the demand in translators is not so high anymore.

One day my eye caught a book in a book store, written by John Dackett. It was called HTML and CSS. Development and creation of websites.

Of course, I bought it! And since then my life has changed. I realised that it was something that would bring me great satisfaction and feeling of self-fullfillment. This book is the reason that you are reading these words now!
